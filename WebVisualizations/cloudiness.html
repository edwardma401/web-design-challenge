Latitude Vs. Cloudiness

The above plot is describing the relationship between latitude and cloudiness in a day of June 2020. Noticably, cities with latitude higher than 20 recorded majority of cloudiness. Hence, the importance of this plot is that it found the pattern of cloudiness in relation to the latitude in the day of observation. The cities with latitude higher than 20 (or above equator) tended to have higher cloudiness.
